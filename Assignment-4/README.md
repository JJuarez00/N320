# Assignment 4: BeatFlow — Music Player Dashboard

## The Situation:
You are a junior developer at BeatFlow, a music streaming startup. The company is going through a major restructuring — layoffs are happening across every team. Your manager gives you one chance to prove your value: build the internal music player feature that the product team has been waiting on for months.

If you ship it, you keep your job. If you don't — well, there are twenty other developers who would love your desk.

The UI and styles are already done. The HTML structure and CSS are provided. All you need to do is write the JavaScript — using the Object-Oriented Programming patterns you have learned — to make it all work.

No pressure. Good luck.
